Mar del Plata, Argentina

Mar del Plata is a city in the province of Buenos Aires, Argentina. It's in the coast of the Atlantic Ocean. It's strong at tourism, prepared with good prices and plenty of activities and restaurants for visitors. Culturally, it's one of the most important cities in Argentina with many theaters and museums. There are many industries too, the main one is the fishing industry. Its alfajores are famous throughout the country.
