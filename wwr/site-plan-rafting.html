Overview

Purpose

With our experimented guides, adventures will be able to have a safety experience getting down the river. They will learn techniques of rafting, about the river and its natural richness, and about conditions of the enviroment for this sport.

Audience

The customers are mainly young adults in their twenties and thirties. They are looking for good options in outside groupal activities and sports. They're going to find in the site: the programs, the squedules, the costs, our contact info, our social networks, info about our company and the experience we have in this area. They usually acces the website by movile device.
